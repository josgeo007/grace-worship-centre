About Us

Aim:Through our fellowship, we aim to nurture and support individuals on their spiritual journey towards salvation.

Mission & Vision: Salvation of souls.We strive to create a welcoming and interactive environment where individuals can grow spiritually and find solace in their relationship with God. Our church is committed to providing a casual and engaging atmosphere for those in search of peace and a deeper connection with the Lord.

Grace Worship Centre is a Christ-centered church committed to worship, prayer, and community outreach. We believe in the power of God’s Word to transform lives.

Grace Worship Centre Nanded operates under Ark International Ministries, led by Pastor T.M. George.
